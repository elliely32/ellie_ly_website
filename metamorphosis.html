METAMORPHOSIS

Software/Materials Used to Make The Project: Paint Tool Sai, Paper, Pencil

Metamorphosis is a web comic adaptation of the song Metamorphosis by Infinity Song. It follows the story of a little girl who has a dream she wants to achieve but struggles through insecurities and doubt while trying to make it come true.

When I first heard this song, it felt like a heavy weight was lifted off my shoulders. In my own journey through art, I was going through a lot of struggles and tribulations. I failed myself, experienced imposter syndrome, and had many moments where I just wanted to give it all up. It gives me solace to know that I'm not the only one going through it.

Although the fears are still there, I hope that creating this little story puts me one step closer to recognizing my worth and realizing my dreams.
